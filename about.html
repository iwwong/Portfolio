HI, I'M IRIS!

I’m a fifth year student at Simon Fraser majoring in Interactive Arts & Technology and minoring in Print and Digital Publishing. My primary area of focus is Visual Design, I love collaborating with others to work towards simple yet, meaningful designs.

I’m also a social media coordinator for my food photography Instagram, Guacarons where I share my visually appealing images of food and collaborate with restaurants and companies to create strategic promotional content.
