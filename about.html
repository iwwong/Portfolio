HI, I'M IRIS!

I’m a visual designer and social media coordinator from Vancouver, Canada. Previously, I worked at Simon Fraser University (SFU) as a Design and Marketing Coordinator.

I'm really passionate about creating simple yet, meaningful designs. Currently, I'm a fifth year student at SFU majoring in Interactive Arts & Technology and minoring in Print and Digital Publishing.

I’m also a social media coordinator for my food photography Instagram, Guacarons where I share visually appealing images of food and collaborate with restaurants and companies to create strategic promotional content.
